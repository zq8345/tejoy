For Starlink Gen 3 Mount, Starlink Pivot Mount with Pipe Adapter and Mast, Swiveling Mechanism Design, Standard Gen 3 Mount for Slanted, shingled Roofs, Easy to Install and Adjust.

Contact Now Back


Starlink Gen 3 Heavy Duty Pivot Roof Mount, designed to withstand severe weather, allows for easy angle adjustment to ensure optimal signal reception.

Features:

  • Adjustable Pivot Mount - Starlink Gen 3 Pivot Mount with 180-degree swivel pivot and 360-degree rotation mast, provides omnidirectional adjustment for your Starlink Gen 3 on the roof to adapt to various angles and directions, helping locate the best signal from the sky

  • Solid Aluminum Construction - Crafted by heavy-duty die-casting aluminum alloy for sturdiness and rust-resistance, the pivot mount withstands extreme weather conditions from snow, hail, sleet, heavy rain to extreme heat, providing a stable mount for the dish

  • Easy Setup - Designed for straightforward mounting to wood roof, simply attach the mount to the roof surface and click the dish into the mount, adjust the tilt angle, and it’s ready to use

  • User-friendly Design - Boasts a large pivot knob that allows for easy manual adjustment without any tools, simply twist the knob to secure the desired angle

  • Enhanced Stability - Tightening knobs on the pivot, mast, and mast adapter secure the mount firmly in place, ensuring stronger grip and enhanced stability

Specifications:

  • Overall Dimension(LxWxH): 8 11/16" x 5 1/2" x 14 9/16" (22 x 14 x 37 cm)

  • Mast Adapter Dimension: 7 1/2" x 5 5/16" x 3 1/8" (19 x 13.5 x 8 cm)

  • Pivot Mount Dimension(LxWxH): 8 7/8" x 5 1/2" x 6 11/16" (22.5 x 14 x 17 cm)

  • Compatibility: Starlink Standard Gen 3

  • Material: Aluminum Alloy, Glass-fiber Reinforced Polycarbonate

  • Net Weight: 3 Lbs (1.35 kg)

Package Includes:

  • 1x Mast Adapter

  • 1x Mast

  • 1x Pivot Mount

  • 2x Screws

  • 20x Single Cable Screw Clips

  • 1x Allen Key

  • 1x Manual
