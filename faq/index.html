FAQ

Answers on compatibility, certification, warranty and OEM/ODM.

Do your products have international certifications (such as FCC, CE, RoHS)? ▼

Yes. Our products are tested and certified to major international standards, including FCC (US emission standards), CE (European standards), and RoHS (restriction of hazardous substances). Every product undergoes rigorous testing to meet these standards. Full details are available on our Certifications & Testing page.

Are your products fully compatible with Starlink official equipment? ▼

All our accessories undergo real-world testing and verification with Starlink equipment during the R&D phase to ensure compatibility and physical and electrical stability compatibility with Starlink terminals (such as MINI/Gen2/Gen3).

How durable are your outdoor protective accessories (such as protective covers/mounting brackets)? ▼

All accessories are made from premium materials, eliminating low-quality products, and are suitable for long-term deployment in most harsh outdoor environments.

Will cables/extension cords affect signal transmission quality? ▼

We use low-loss cables and shielding technology to ensure signal attenuation is below industry standards, and provide actual test data reports and videos.

Do you support OEM/ODM customization services? ▼

Supported. Customization of accessory specifications, logo printing, packaging, etc., is available upon request. Please consult our sales team for minimum order quantity and delivery time. We also welcome technical exchanges and collaborative product development.

Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ) requirement? ▼

The standard product MOQ is 100 pieces. Customized orders require evaluation based on the proposed solution. Small trial orders are negotiable.

Where are your main shipping warehouses located? What logistics methods do you support? ▼

We have a warehousing center in the United States, supporting DHL/FedEx sea and air freight. We offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) service; specific delivery time and costs are calculated based on the destination.

How do you handle the delivery stability of large-scale orders? ▼

We employ a segmented production and multi-warehouse inventory strategy to ensure flexible production capacity and provide real-time order progress tracking reports.

What is the product quality guarantee period? ▼

The standard warranty period is 12 months. For quality issues not caused by human error, returns and exchanges can be requested within 30 days of receiving the goods. Specific terms will be agreed upon and a service agreement signed before cooperation.

Do you provide technical installation guidance or after-sales support? ▼

We provide multilingual installation videos and technical documents. Large clients can schedule remote commissioning or on-site training.

Do you provide price protection policies for distributors/agents? ▼

Yes, we provide regional price protection, marketing materials, and sales training for our core partners. Specific policies require a cooperation agreement.

Can you provide product liability insurance or compliance certificates? ▼

We can provide product liability insurance certificates and compliance documents to assist buyers in handling import inspections.

Are the accessories suitable for specific industries (maritime, mining, emergency rescue)? ▼

We develop specialized accessories for different environments (such as anti-salt spray marine kits, high vibration-resistant mining supports) and provide industry solution case studies.

How do you handle Starlink equipment model iterations? ▼

We closely monitor Starlink hardware updates, conduct compatibility testing before new product launches, and provide upgrade solutions for existing customers.

Can you provide past cooperation cases or customer recommendations? ▼

We have served over 50 operators, system integrators, and government projects worldwide.
