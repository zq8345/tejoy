Upgrade Your Power Setup with Our High-Efficiency Mini Flat Cable

Experience seamless, high-speed power delivery with our Mini Flat Power Charging Cable, engineered for modern electronics that demand both performance and portability. Available in two versatile configurations, this cable is the ultimate connector solution for all your mini device needs.

Dual Version Options for Maximum Compatibility:

Type-C to DC: Transform your PD wall adapter into a universal DC power source, perfect for connecting laptops, monitors, or projectors with DC inputs.

DC to DC: Extend existing DC power setups effortlessly, ideal for LED strips, portable fans, or networking equipment requiring a reliable barrel jack connection.

Key Product Features:

High-Power Performance:

Supports up to 60W (20V 3A) power transfer, ensuring rapid charging and stable energy supply for power-hungry gadgets without compromising safety or speed.

Space-Saving Flat Design:

The ultra-slim, flat cable construction eliminates frustrating tangles and knots. Its compact profile allows for easy cable management—route it behind desks, through tight spaces, or carry it conveniently in your tech bag.

Industrial-Grade Durability:

Features precision-molded DC connectors with reinforced stress relief. The secure grip design prevents accidental disconnections during use, while the robust build ensures longevity for daily wear and tear.

Universal Mini Connector Accessory:

Whether you need a replacement cable or an extension, this power charging cable serves as the perfect accessory for home offices, travel, or industrial applications. Its sleek black finish blends seamlessly with any device aesthetic.

Specifications:

Max Power: 60W

Output: 20V / 3A

Cable Style: Flat, Tangle-Free

Connector Types: USB Type-C Male to DC Male / DC Male to DC Male (Select Version)

Compatibility: Devices with DC barrel jack inputs (Please verify plug dimensions: 5.5×2.1mm or 5.5×2.5mm)
