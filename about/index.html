About Us

Engineering-led manufacturing since 2010.

Tejoy Starlink Accessories Limited is an engineering-led manufacturer of Starlink-compatible accessories, based in the Guangdong–Hong Kong–Macao Greater Bay Area. Under one integrated R&D, production and sales operation, we design and build a full range of peripherals for Starlink terminals — waterproof network and power cables, mounts and brackets, power adapters, protective cases, and signal-expansion hardware.

Every product is engineered to meet the weather-resistance and outdoor demands of Starlink Mini, Standard, and Performance (Gen 1/2/3) terminals, giving global buyers a stable, durable, and precisely fitted one-stop supply. Tejoy is an independent, third-party manufacturer and is not affiliated with SpaceX or Starlink.
