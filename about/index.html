About Us

TEJOY STARLINK ACCESSORIES LIMITED. is a high-tech enterprise focusing on the R&D, production and sales of satellite chain terminal supporting products, based on the scientific innovation highland of Guangdong Hong Kong Macao Greater Bay Area and following the global satellite Internet industry tide. The company focuses on the Musk Starlink equipment ecosystem, with the satellite communication supporting field as the core track, adhering to independent innovation and precision manufacturing. It has formed a full industry chain operation system with independent research and development, self owned factories, and integrated research, production, and sales. It is a professional solution provider and core manufacturer of Starlink ecological supporting accessories products.

The company has a standardized production plant of 5000 square meters, a modern independent office area, and more than 100 employees. It is equipped with modern automatic production lines and professional testing equipment, with a monthly production capacity of 400000 to 600000 pieces, and has stable production capacity with scale and high efficiency. The core main products include Star Chain series dedicated peripheral accessories, including Gen2/Gen3/MINI waterproof network cables, DC power cables, car cables, outdoor extension cables, as well as supporting power adapters, installation brackets, protective shells, signal expansion equipment, storage bags, etc Accurately adapting to the weather resistance requirements and outdoor application scenarios of SpaceX Starlink terminal equipment, providing a stable, durable, and highly adaptable one-stop supporting solution for global Starlink users.

With excellent compatibility, stability, and durability of its products, the company's products are exported to multiple countries and regions around the world, and its annual export revenue remains stable in the range of 20-30 million US dollars, deeply recognized and trusted by domestic and foreign partners and customers. Facing new opportunities for the development of the global satellite internet industry, TEJOY STARLINK ACCESSORIES LIMITED. will continue to be driven by technological innovation, supported by strong research and development capabilities, and rooted in intelligent manufacturing. We will deeply cultivate the field of Starlink ecological support, continuously enhance our product core competitiveness and brand global influence, and strive to become a leading professional service provider for Starlink network accessories worldwide.
