Starlink Mount Compatibility Guide: Mini vs Standard vs Performance

  • 2026-06-28

Which Starlink Mount Do You Need?

Choosing the right mount for your Starlink terminal depends on your terminal type and installation scenario. Wanew offers compatible mounting solutions for all Starlink terminal generations.

Mount Types by Terminal

Starlink Mini Mounts

The Mini terminal is smaller and lighter, making it ideal for portable and RV use. Wanew offers:

  • Roof mounts for RV and van installations
  • Portable tripod mounts for camping
  • Pipe mounts for fixed installations

Standard Terminal Mounts

For Standard (Round), Standard Actuated, and Standard Circular terminals, Wanew provides heavy-duty mounting options suitable for permanent residential and commercial installations.

Performance Terminal Mounts

Performance Gen 1, 2, and 3 terminals require robust mounting for high-wind environments. Our mounts are engineered for industrial-grade durability.

Installation Considerations

  • Roof Mounts: Best for permanent RV and home installation. Requires weather sealing.
  • Pipe Mounts: Versatile for ground, wall, or pole mounting. Suitable for most terminal types.
  • Adjustable Mounts: Ideal for fine-tuning alignment in areas with obstructions.
