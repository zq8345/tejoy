1. Quality Philosophy

At Wanew, quality is not a department — it is integrated into every stage of production. Our goal is zero defects delivered to the customer. We achieve this through rigorous incoming inspection, in-process monitoring, final functional testing, and continuous improvement based on field data.

Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001:2015, audited annually by SGS. We maintain a quality defect rate below 0.5% across all product lines, verified by third-party inspections.

We stand behind every product. If a defect is found, we replace it within 15 business days — no questions asked.

3. 4-Stage QC Process

Stage 1: Incoming Quality Control (IQC)

MaterialInspectionAcceptance Criteria
Steel / AluminumDimensional, material grade verification, surface finishANSI/ASQ Z1.4 AQL 1.0
Plastic ResinMelt flow index, color match, impact resistanceSupplier COA + in-house verification
Cables / WireConductor gauge, insulation thickness, continuity100% on critical lots
Hardware (bolts, nuts)Thread gauge, hardness test, plating thicknessANSI/ASME B1.1 Class 2A

Stage 2: In-Process QC (IPQC)

  • First Article Inspection (FAI) at start of every production run
  • Patrol inspection every 2 hours: dimensional, visual, assembly fit
  • Sampling rate: 20% for critical dimensions, 10% for visual
  • Any defect triggers 100% inspection of the preceding batch
  • Process capability monitoring (CpK) for critical processes

Stage 3: Final Quality Control (FQC)

  • 100% functional testing for all products:
  • Mounts: Fit test with standard Starlink base plate and hardware
  • Adapters: Load test at rated current for 30 minutes
  • Cables: Continuity, insulation resistance (>100 M Ohm)
  • Assemblies: Torque test on all fasteners
  • Dimensional inspection: Sampling per AQL 2.5
  • Visual inspection: 100% for surface defects, scratches, discoloration

Stage 4: Outgoing Quality Control (OQC)

  • Statistical sampling per AQL 2.5 (normal), AQL 1.0 (critical items)
  • Packaging integrity check: label accuracy, barcode scan, seal inspection
  • Quantity verification: 100% count for orders under 10,000 units
  • Documentation: Certificate of Conformance, inspection report, test results
  • Third-party inspection available upon request (SGS, Bureau Veritas, TUV)

Zero-Defect Policy: Critical defects are not tolerated at any sampling level. If a single critical defect is found in a lot, the entire lot is quarantined and 100% inspected. The root cause is investigated and corrected before production resumes.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I request third-party inspection of my order?

A: Yes. We welcome third-party inspections from SGS, Bureau Veritas, TUV Rheinland, or any accredited inspection agency. The cost is borne by the buyer. We provide full access to production lines and finished goods for inspection.

What happens if a defect is found after shipment?

A: Notify us within 30 days of receipt. We will request photos or returned samples for analysis. If confirmed as a manufacturing defect, we issue replacement or credit within 15 business days. Shipping cost for replacements is borne by Wanew.

Do you provide test reports with each shipment?

A: Yes. Each shipment includes a Certificate of Conformance (CoC) signed by our QC manager. Upon request, we also provide the Inspection Report (dimensional, functional, visual) and material certifications.
