5. Customs & Duties

DDP Service — Hassle-Free Customs

With our DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) service to the US and EU, we handle everything:

  • Customs clearance in the destination country
  • Payment of all import duties and taxes (included in the quoted price)
  • Door-to-door delivery with full tracking
  • No broker fees, no surprise charges, no customs paperwork on your end

7. US Warehouse & DDP Service

For our North American customers, Wanew maintains a US warehouse that provides several advantages:

  • Fast domestic delivery: 1-3 business days across the continental US
  • No international shipping costs: Pay domestic freight only (from US warehouse)
  • No customs clearance: All duties and taxes pre-paid
  • Easy returns: Return defective products to our US address, not to China
  • Low minimums: Order as few as 50-100 units from US stock

Our DDP service (Delivered Duty Paid) is available for both sea and air shipments to the US and EU. You receive one all-inclusive price — no hidden fees at customs, no broker charges, no surprise duties.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer sample shipping?

A: Yes. Samples are shipped via DHL or FedEx Express. The sample cost is at our cost price (no markup), and shipping is at your expense. Sample shipping cost is typically $30-$50 to most destinations.

What is the best shipping method for first-time orders?

A: For first-time orders under 20 kg, we recommend DDP Express (DHL/FedEx with delivery duty paid). You pay one price and receive the goods at your door with no customs involvement. For larger orders, DDP air or DDP sea freight work well.

How are orders packaged for export?

A: All export shipments are professionally packed in corrugated cartons with proper cushioning. Large orders are palletized and shrink-wrapped. Each carton is labeled with product details, quantity, and handling instructions.
